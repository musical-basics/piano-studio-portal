# Trial Lesson Confirmation Email

For new prospects who book a free 15-minute trial lesson.

**Goals of this email:**
1. Confirm the booking and reduce no-shows (free trials are high-risk for flakes).
2. Set expectations for the 15 minutes so it isn't wasted on logistics.
3. Tell them how to prepare so the call is actually useful.
4. Plant the enrollment hook without being pushy.

---

## Subject Line Options

- Your free trial lesson is confirmed — here's how to prep
- See you [Day] at [Time] — your trial lesson with Lionel
- Trial lesson confirmed: [Day, Date] at [Time]

(Pick one. The first is the strongest for open rates.)

---

## Email Body

Hi [First Name],

Looking forward to meeting you. Your free 15-minute trial lesson is confirmed for:

**[Day, Date] at [Time] [Timezone]**
[Zoom link]
Meeting ID: [ID]
Passcode: [Passcode]

### What we'll do in 15 minutes

This is a short call, so I want to make every minute count. Here's what to expect:

1. **A quick conversation about your goals.** What you want to learn, what you've tried, where you're stuck.
2. **A short play-through.** If you already play, bring something you've been working on — even if it's rough. If you're brand new, that's fine too.
3. **A clear next step.** I'll tell you whether semester enrollment makes sense for you, and if so, what that would look like.

### How to prep

- **Be at your piano or keyboard.** Acoustic is great, digital is fine. Just not your phone speaker.
- **Test your camera and mic before the call.** Zoom likes to surprise people. Two-minute test saves us five minutes of troubleshooting.
- **Have something ready to play, if you play.** A piece, an exercise, a few measures — whatever you've got. Doesn't need to be polished.
- **Find a quiet spot.** Background noise is the #1 thing that wastes trial time.

### If you need to reschedule

Just reply to this email. No questions asked, no awkwardness — I'd rather move it than have us both show up tired.

### A bit about what comes after

If we're a fit, quarterly enrollment opens up access to lesson recordings, custom sheet music, twice-yearly online recitals, my full masterclass library (100+ lessons), and 12 lesson credits per quarter. We'll talk through it on the call if it makes sense. No pressure on the trial.

See you soon,

Lionel
Lionel Yu Piano Studio
lessons.musicalbasics.com

## Sender / Setup Notes

- **From name:** Lionel Yu (not "Lionel Yu Piano Studio") — personal name converts better for a one-on-one trial.
- **Reply-to:** A real address you actually check, not a no-reply. Trial students often reply with questions.
- **Send timing:** Immediately on booking, plus a reminder 24 hours before and a second reminder 1 hour before. (The 1-hour reminder is the single biggest no-show reducer for free calls.)
- **Calendar invite:** Attach an .ics file. Don't make them manually add it.
